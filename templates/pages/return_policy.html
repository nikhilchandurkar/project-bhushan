Return Policy

Understanding our return and exchange policy

Last Updated: November 2025

No Returns or Exchanges

Please note: We do not accept returns or exchanges on any products purchased from our store. All sales are final. We encourage our customers to carefully review product descriptions, specifications, and features before making a purchase decision.

Why We Have This Policy

As a small electronics retail store focused on providing the most affordable prices to our customers in Malegaon, we operate on minimal margins. To keep our prices competitive and accessible, we maintain a no-return policy. This allows us to offer you the best possible prices on quality electronics and accessories.

Before You Purchase

To ensure you're completely satisfied with your purchase, we recommend:

  • Read Product Descriptions Carefully: Review all specifications, features, and compatibility information
  • Check Product Images: Examine all product images to understand what you're purchasing
  • Verify Compatibility: Ensure the product is compatible with your device or requirements
  • Contact Us First: If you have any questions or doubts, please reach out to us before placing your order
  • Visit Our Store: You're welcome to visit our physical store to see products in person

Need Help Choosing?

Our customer support team is here to help! Contact us at +91 98765 43210 or info@yourstore.com before making a purchase. We're happy to answer your questions and help you choose the right product for your needs.

Defective or Damaged Products

While we do not accept returns for change of mind, we understand that manufacturing defects or shipping damage can occur. If you receive a defective or damaged product:

  • Report Within 48 Hours: Contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order
  • Provide Evidence: Send us clear photographs or videos showing the defect or damage
  • Keep Packaging: Retain all original packaging, accessories, and documentation
  • Product Condition: The product must be unused and in its original condition

Upon verification, we will assess the situation and may offer a replacement subject to product availability. This is handled on a case-by-case basis at our sole discretion.

Manufacturer Warranty

Many of our products come with manufacturer warranties. These warranties are separate from our return policy and are governed by the manufacturer's terms and conditions.

  • Keep your purchase invoice safe - it's required for warranty claims
  • Warranty periods and terms vary by manufacturer and product
  • We will assist you in facilitating warranty claims with manufacturers
  • Contact us for guidance on the warranty claim process

What Is NOT Covered

We cannot offer replacements or solutions for:

  • Change of mind or buyer's remorse
  • Incorrect product ordered by customer
  • Damage caused by misuse, abuse, or improper handling after delivery
  • Products damaged due to water, physical impact, or unauthorized repairs
  • Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect product functionality
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Issues reported after 48 hours of delivery
  • Products without original packaging or missing accessories

Order Cancellation

Once an order is placed and confirmed, it cannot be cancelled. Please review your order carefully before completing the purchase. Orders that have not yet been confirmed or processed may be eligible for cancellation - contact us immediately if you need to cancel a recent order.

Inspection Upon Delivery

We strongly recommend that you inspect your order immediately upon delivery:

  • Check for any visible damage to the packaging
  • Verify that you've received the correct products
  • Ensure all items and accessories are included
  • Test the product functionality as soon as possible
  • Report any issues within 48 hours

How to Report an Issue

If you receive a defective or damaged product, follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us Immediately: Call us at +91 98765 43210 or email info@yourstore.com within 48 hours
  2. Provide Order Details: Include your order number, product name, and purchase date
  3. Submit Evidence: Send clear photos or videos showing the defect or damage from multiple angles
  4. Wait for Assessment: Our team will review your case and respond within 1-2 business days

Our Commitment to Quality

While we maintain a no-return policy, we are committed to customer satisfaction. We carefully inspect all products before shipping and only source from reputable manufacturers and suppliers. Our goal is to provide you with quality products at affordable prices, along with excellent customer service.
